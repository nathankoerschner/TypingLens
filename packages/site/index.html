TypingLens
How it works Mac app

A typing coach for proficient typists

Type better by fixing what actually slows you down.

TypingLens watches your typing locally on your Mac, ranks the words and patterns costing you speed and accuracy, and turns them into drills shaped by your own history.

Download for Mac

Local only. No account. macOS 13+.

How it works

Capture, rank, practice.

  1. 1

    Capture local typing data

    TypingLens records your typing activity locally on your Mac.

  2. 2

    Rank what slows you down

    It surfaces the words and patterns most associated with lost speed and mistakes.

  3. 3

    Practice against real weaknesses

    Drills and analytics come from your history, not from generic word lists.

The Mac app

Built around how you actually type.

TypingLens is a SwiftUI app for macOS 13 and up. It keeps your typing data on your machine and practice adapts as your typing changes.
